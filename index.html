GameFeed is a web application for discovering new games based on player preferences of genre, cost, development companies, ratings, and more.

Whether it be searching for your next gaming adventure or just looking around, GameFeed can get you the most up to date recommendations!

Currently, users can only search for games by title. Additional features may be added in the future.
